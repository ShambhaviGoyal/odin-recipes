Homemade Sushi Recipe

Learn how to make delicious sushi at home with this easy-to-follow guide. Includes sushi rice, sushi rolls, and helpful tips!

Ingredients

Sushi Rice

  • 2 cups sushi rice
  • 2 1/2 cups water (for cooking the rice)
  • 1/4 cup rice vinegar
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt

Sushi Rolls (Example Rolls)

  • 1 sheet nori (seaweed)
  • 1/2 cucumber, julienned
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 200g sushi-grade tuna or salmon (optional for fish sushi)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce (for dipping)
  • 1 tbsp wasabi (optional, for extra spice)
  • Pickled ginger (for serving)

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Sushi Rice

  1. Rinse the sushi rice under cold water until the water runs clear to remove excess starch.
  2. In a rice cooker or pot, cook the rice with 2 1/2 cups of water according to the rice cooker's instructions or bring it to a boil on the stove and then simmer for about 10-12 minutes, covered.
  3. While the rice is cooking, mix the rice vinegar, sugar, and salt in a small bowl until dissolved.
  4. Once the rice is done, transfer it to a large bowl. While it's still hot, gently fold in the vinegar mixture to season the rice. Let the rice cool to room temperature.

Step 2: Prepare the Rolls

  1. Place a bamboo sushi mat on a clean surface. Lay a sheet of nori (shiny side down) on the mat.
  2. Using wet hands (to prevent sticking), spread a thin layer of sushi rice over the nori, leaving about an inch at the top of the nori sheet.
  3. Place your fillings (cucumber, avocado, fish) in a line across the center of the rice.
  4. Carefully lift the edge of the sushi mat closest to you and start rolling tightly, using the mat to guide the roll. Once you reach the exposed edge of the nori, moisten it with a bit of water to seal the roll.
  5. Use a sharp knife to slice the roll into bite-sized pieces. Clean the knife between each slice to make it easier to cut.

Step 3: Serve and Enjoy

  1. Serve your sushi with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger on the side for an authentic experience!
  2. Enjoy your homemade sushi with family and friends!
